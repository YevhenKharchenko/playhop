How to Play

  • 1

    Pick a Game

    Choose from a selection of Oink Games like Deep Sea Adventure or A Fake Artist Goes to New York.

  • 2

    Join or Create a Room

    Invite friends or join random players online

  • 3

    Play & Strategize

    Use logic, bluffing, or teamwork depending on the game rules!

  • 4

    Earn Points

    Win games and rise through the global leaderboard!
